INSECTS

Aphids

Aphids feed on the sap from the leaves, which can cause them to curl, distort, or become misshapen.

Brown string

The Brown Marmorated Stink Bug feeds by piercing the fruit with its mouthparts and sucking out the juices.

Anthracnose Leaf

Anthracnose causes dark lesions on apple leaves and fruit, leading to premature fruit drop. Manage it by improving air circulation and applying fungicides.

Alternaria Leaf

Alternaria causes dark spots on apples and leaves, leading to damage and drop. Manage it with better air circulation and fungicide




The AGventure system integrates drone technology to improve apple orchard management and drones equipped with advanced imaging systems (multispectral and thermal) monitor tree health, detect water stress, and identify early signs of disease. They also enable precision application of fertilizers and pesticides, reducing waste and environmental impact. Additionally, drones help estimate apple production by analyzing blossom counts and fruit set, improving planning and logistics. Finally, drone footage is used to create virtual tours for marketing, and blockchain ensures product traceability, enhancing the visibility and profitability of Himachal apples.
